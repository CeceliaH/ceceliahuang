Title: The Penguin and the Purple Flower

Medium: Digital

Description: This piece is a still-life drawing of a stuffed animal with some fantastical elements. I used different brushes on Photoshop to make the penguin as realistic-looking as possible and used dodge and burn tools to create the flower. I also used chiaroscuro to highlight the penguin and to add more interest into this still-life piece.
